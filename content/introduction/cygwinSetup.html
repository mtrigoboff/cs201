CS201: Cygwin Setup

To get Cygwin, go to the Cygwin home page and run (or download and then run) the Cygwin installer.

Optional Items You Will Need

When you get to the “Select Packages” page of the installer, choose the following:

  • Devel section:
    • gcc-core
    • gdb
    • make
  • Editors section – one or both of:
    • vim (this is Cygwin’s version of vi)
    • emacs
  • Net section
    • openssh

You can always run the Cygwin installer again and modify your Cygwin setup, either adding or deleting packages.

Your home directory (if you accepted the installer’s default) is C:\cygwin\home\Your Username
