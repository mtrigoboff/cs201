About the Virtual Proctoring Tool

Please review the minimum technology requirements listed below for taking your exam with a live online proctor:

  • A well-working computer with 1 GB of RAM or higher.
  • A high-speed internet connection (3 Mbps).  (A wired connection is ideal.)
  • Any webcam with 640x480 video pixel resolution (a laptop camera is acceptable).
  • Working speakers connected to the computer (laptop speakers are acceptable.)
  • A microphone connected to the computer (laptop microphone is acceptable.)
  • Flash player version 7 or higher.
  • Browser compatibility: IE, Firefox, Chrome, Safari.

You can view a brief video demonstration of the system. (This video was created for a different school so you will see their branding, but the tool functions in the same way for PCC students.)

Ready to schedule?

If you have decided to make use of BVirtual for your online exam, please follow these instructions:

  1. When you are ready to schedule your exam session, click on the registration link to register. Remember to schedule your exam session well in advance of the exam week.
  2. Notify your instructor regarding the date and time you have scheduled your exam.
