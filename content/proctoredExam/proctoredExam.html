CS201: Proctored Exam

Important Exam Information

This course will have one proctored exam (the final exam). The exam must be taken either in person with your instructor at the on campus session, or online using the virtual proctoring option. If neither of these options work for you, you must contact your instructor to discuss proctoring alternatives.

As your instructor, I will be on campus for the final exam on the following dates: 

  • The in-class section (crn 30193) final exam is on August 24
    from 10:00 AM until 12:00 PM on the Sylvania Campus in room TCB 311.
  • The distance learning section (crn 34295) final exam is on August 25
    from 10:00 AM until 12:00 PM on the Sylvania Campus in room TCB 310.

All students who are able to attend the instructor proctored session are required to do so. 

If you are unable to attend the instructor proctored session due to time or location constraints, you may make use the the BVirtual online proctoring system. Other proctoring accommodations may be approved by your instructor on an individual basis.
